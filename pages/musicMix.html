Project overview

MusicMix is free digital music service that provides access to over 30 million songs with about 20 thousand songs being added daily and 100 million active users. MusicMix is available on both android and IOS devices however it is locally bound to definite regions. .

The problem :
Available just within a small number of countires. One of the biggest flaws in many video and music streaming services is the fact that a limited number of countires can only have a access to the same goes with MusicMix.
The goal:
Design We envision a cultural platform where professional creators can break free of their medium’s constraints and where everyone can enjoy an immersive artistic experience that enables us to empathize with each other and to feel part of a greater whole.
